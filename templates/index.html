Personalized Zoom Link

Follow the steps below to retrieve your Zoom Personal Meeting Room URL:

  1. Log in to your Zoom account.
  2. In Settings, go to Meetings on the left of the page.
  3. Under the "Personal Room" section, copy the Invite Link.
  4. Enter your first name in the Name field.
  5. Paste the Link in the Link field.
  6. Press Submit!
