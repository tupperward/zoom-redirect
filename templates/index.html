Follow the steps below to retrieve your Zoom Personal Meeting Room URL:

  1. Be a Ward, otherwise this is weird.
  2. Log in to your Zoom account.
  3. Navigate to the "Settings" page.
  4. Under the "Personal Meeting Room" section, copy the URL.
  5. Enter your first name in the Name field.
  6. Paste the URL in the URL field.
  7. Press Submit!
